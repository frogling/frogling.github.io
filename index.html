Frogling is a free android application that allows you to share your thoughts with your closest friends and even the whole world, using just a simple picture with several words. Basically, all you have to do is just find yourself in an interesting situation, enter the app and post a frogling (a picture with words) about it. After all, a picture speaks a thousand words, so imagine how much froglings can say!


Features
  • Take a picture or upload photos from your gallery
  • Powerful editing tools
  • Browse froglings posted by your friends and people around the world
  • A reliable search engine
  • Find froglins using our categories and references to recent events
